Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions


What is the Visual Group Gold Scheme?
The Visual Group Gold Scheme is an exclusive monthly savings plan from Visual Group Associates where members save ₹1,000 per month and get automatic entries into lucky draws for premium prizes like cars, homes, gold, and electronics.
How do I join the scheme and participate in lucky draws?
You can join by registering through our authorized centers or representatives and beginning your ₹1,000 monthly contribution. Every active member is automatically entered into our lucky draws based on eligibility.
What kind of prizes are given in the lucky draws?
Winners can receive a wide range of prizes including cars, 2BHK homes, bikes, gold coins, household appliances, mobile phones, and more — as per the draw schedule.
How will I know if I’ve won a prize?
Winners are personally informed through SMS or call. Results are also published on our official website, social media pages, and displayed at our centers.
Is the lucky draw system transparent?
Absolutely. All draws are conducted publicly using certified randomization systems, often in the presence of legal observers and members for full transparency.
Can I stop or withdraw from the scheme at any time?
While the scheme is designed for committed savers, you may exit under certain conditions. Please refer to our terms & conditions or speak with your branch representative for details.
How do I reach Visual Group’s support team?
You can contact us through the Contact Us section on our website, call our helpline, or visit your nearest Visual Group branch for any assistance.
